The water crisis affects over a quarter of the world population. We're joining the big struggle to broaden access to clean drinking water for all, so that the true potential of our world population can be realized.

Employing techniques researched by scientists across the globe, we seek to harvest water in fog, making water scarcity scarcely a problem for those in humid environments.
