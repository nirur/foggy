Mission

We aim to provide an affordable and sustainable water supply for those in water-scarce regions. Concretely, our design goals are:
  • Be as cheap as possible. As a baseline, we must be cheaper than bottled water in the long-term.
  • Be able to sell to (nearly) any willing customer. This includes small-scale portable units, units for family use, and large units for cities.
  • Be long-lasting: customers shouldn't need to replace these units for at least a month, ideally longer.
  • Be low-maintenance. During regular use, besides collecting water, the customer shouldn't need to interact with the system. Maintenance should be necessary at most once a month.
